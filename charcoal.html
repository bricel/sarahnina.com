Sarah Nina Meridor

CHARCOAL

Discovering essence through the pure language of light and shadow

The CHARCOAL series strips away color to reveal the fundamental dialogue between light and darkness. In this monochromatic exploration, each mark carries the weight of intention, each gradation speaks to the subtle transitions between being and non-being.

Working with charcoal - carbon in its purest form - connects the work to the elemental building blocks of life itself. The medium's natural tendency to smudge and blend mirrors the fluid boundaries between the conscious and unconscious, the formed and the formless.

These drawings emerge from a meditative process where the hand follows intuition, where the conscious mind steps aside to allow deeper currents of expression to flow. Each piece is both a record of this process and an invitation for the viewer to enter their own contemplative space.
