Sarah Nina Meridor

Artist's Statement

On the intersection of the mystical and the material

In the vast expanse of artistic expression, I, as a humble vessel, seek to merge the celestial and terrestrial realms through my creative endeavor. In each stroke of paint upon the canvas, I dance with the ineffable, allowing the colors to intertwine like whispered secrets from the cosmos.

My work emerges from the liminal space where ancient wisdom meets contemporary consciousness, where the mystical traditions of Jerusalem—my home—converge with the universal language of visual art. Drawing from Kabbalistic concepts, quantum physics, and the phenomenology of perception, I create pieces that serve as bridges between dimensions of understanding.

In my artistry, I aspire to evoke the timeless wisdom and transcendental beauty that dwells within the hearts of humanity. Just as Rumi's poetry weaves mystical tales, my brush caresses the surface, weaving stories of the soul's journey and the eternal dance between light and shadow.

Each series in my body of work explores a different facet of this cosmic dialogue. MATRONITA investigates the divine feminine presence that watches over creation; the Fine-Structure Constant series translates the fundamental constants of physics into visual poetry; IMAGEWORK examines how consciousness transforms perception into reality; CHARCOAL strips away all but the essential dialogue between light and darkness; and TRANSPHERA explores the transparent boundaries between dimensions.

The creative process itself becomes a form of meditation, a direct encounter with the mystery that underlies all existence. I work with layers—both physical and metaphysical—building meaning through transparency, allowing deeper truths to emerge gradually, like stars becoming visible as daylight fades.

Come, wanderer of art, and allow yourself to be transported to realms where the boundaries between the seen and unseen dissolve, and where the language of the heart speaks louder than words. My paintings are invitations—doorways through which the careful observer might glimpse the profound interconnectedness that weaves through all manifestation.

In the sacred silence of creation, I find solace and purpose, knowing that each piece carries within it a fragment of the infinite, waiting to touch the souls of those who dare to look beyond the surface. Art, for me, is not merely representation but revelation—a means of making visible the invisible currents that flow beneath the surface of ordinary experience.

Welcome to my world of artistic exploration, where the mystical meets the material, and where every creation is a prayer offered to the altar of beauty and truth. May these works serve as companions on your own journey of discovery, reminding you of the wonder that surrounds us when we learn to see with the eyes of the heart.

Sarah Nina Meridor
Jerusalem, 2024
