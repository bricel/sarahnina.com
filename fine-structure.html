FINE-STRUCTURE CONSTANT

The intersection of quantum physics and artistic expression

The Fine-Structure Constant series explores the fundamental constants that govern our universe's electromagnetic interactions. This dimensionless number, approximately 1/137, represents one of physics' greatest mysteries - a constant that determines the strength of electromagnetic force.

These works translate the mathematical elegance and profound mystery of quantum mechanics into visual language, exploring how the invisible forces that bind atoms together might manifest in color, form, and texture.

Each piece contemplates the question posed by physicists: why does this particular number appear to fine-tune the universe for the existence of complex structures, life, and consciousness?
