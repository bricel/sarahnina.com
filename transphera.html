TRANSPHERA

Navigating the transparent spheres between visible and invisible realms

TRANSPHERA explores the liminal spaces where dimensions intersect and transparency becomes a doorway to deeper understanding. These works investigate the concept of spheres - both physical and metaphysical - that exist in states of partial visibility, revealing and concealing simultaneously.

The title combines "trans" (across, beyond) with "sphera" (sphere), suggesting movement through dimensional boundaries. Each piece in this series attempts to capture the moment of transition, when solid forms become permeable and the impossible becomes visible.

Working with layers of translucent media, these paintings create depth through transparency rather than traditional perspective. Light moves through the work as it might through crystal spheres, bending and refracting meaning as it travels from the surface to the viewer's consciousness.

The series asks: What lies beyond the veil of ordinary perception? How might we see through the apparent solidity of things to perceive their essential transparency?
