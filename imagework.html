Sarah Nina Meridor

IMAGEWORK

The alchemy of visual consciousness and creative perception

IMAGEWORK delves into the mysterious process by which consciousness transforms raw perception into meaningful images. This series explores the liminal space where seeing becomes knowing, where the eye's reception of light becomes the mind's creation of reality.

Each piece investigates the active nature of perception - how we don't simply receive images but continuously construct them through layers of memory, emotion, and interpretation. The work questions where external reality ends and internal creation begins.

Through experimental techniques and layered compositions, these paintings attempt to make visible the invisible work of visual consciousness - the constant, miraculous transformation of photons into meaning, light into insight.
