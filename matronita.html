Sarah Nina Meridor

MATRONITA

Exploring the divine feminine through mystical and contemporary interpretations

The MATRONITA series delves into the concept of the divine feminine presence in Kabbalistic tradition. These works explore themes of spiritual protection, nurturing energy, and the sacred feminine principle that governs creation and sustenance.

Through layered compositions and symbolic imagery, each piece invites contemplation of the eternal dance between the seen and unseen realms, where the maternal divine watches over and guides the cosmic order.
